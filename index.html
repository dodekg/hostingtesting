Introduction

As an active cyclist and a person that hugely relies on bicycle in everyday life, I believe that it is important to care how far cycling infrastructure is developed in a place where we live. It is important topic that often requires public debate because in order to improve cycling conditions for better we need to introduce changes, often big changes - the roots of how our cities are designed.

Any idea that is born needs to be agreed, digested, and requires time to happen in reality. I want to discuss the benefits of cycling as we all know - much greener and healthier means of transport. The more often you repeat an issue, the more people start to think about it, “it is brought to life” and that matters for me the most in this project.
